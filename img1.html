"Reflections 1", Oct. 2019, Digital Photography ...
For this small series, Reflections 1-3, I wanted to photograph the subject purely using reflective items such as mirrors or water surfaces. This series was inspired by Nan Goldin and their use of reflective items in their own photography. The focus of this picture especially is how we're not fully confronting the subject even though they're looking at us, part of the eye is still cut off
