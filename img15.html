"mind yo' business", May 2019, Plaster, Cloth, and baby doll ...
This project was made in collaboration with Ashley Magana. We took a cast of her chest and then created a plaster piece from it. I then used a quilt I had actually made back when I was 8 or 9 and decided to stitch "mind yo' business" on it using embroidery thread. We made a statement about how public breastfeeding should be accepted by others, and shluld be viewed as natural.
