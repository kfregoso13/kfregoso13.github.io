"Untitled", Nov. 2019, Digital Photography ...
Visiting antique stores has become sort of a weekend ritual for my mom, aunt, cousin, and I. I decided to take my camera along on one of our trips and ended up with some great shots. In this one you see the surprise on my moms and cousins face when they see a creepy doll that was lying around the store. The clutter in the photo is really what makes this an authentic photo of an antique store.
