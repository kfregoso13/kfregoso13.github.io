"Reflections 2", Oct. 2019, Digital Photography ...
For this small series, Reflections 1-3, I wanted to photograph the subject purely using reflective items such as mirrors or water surfaces. This series was inspired by Nan Goldin and their use of reflective items in their own photography. A part of what makes this picture so successful is the crispness in the reflection that's displayed.
